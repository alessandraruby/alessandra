Duas Collection from the Sunnah

﷽

Dua #1

‎اَللّٰهُمَّ فَقِّهْنِيْ فِيْ الدِّيْنِ

"O Allah! Grant me understanding of Deen." (Bukhari)

Dua #2

‎اَللّٰهُمَّ حَاسِبْنِيْ حِسَابًا يَّسِيْرًا

"O Allah! Grant me an easy reckoning." (Haakim)

Dua #3

‎‎يَا مُقَلِّبَ الْقُلُوْبِ ثَبِّتْ قَلْبِيْ عَلٰى دِيْنِكَ

"O Allah! The Over-turner of hearts, make my heart steadfast and firm upon Your Deen." (Tirmidhi)

Dua #4

‎‎‎يَا مُصَرِّفَ الْقُلُوْبِ صَرِّفْ قَلْبِيْ عَلٰى طَاعَتِكَ

"O Allah! The One who turns the hearts, turn my heart towards Your obedience." (Muslim)

Dua #5

‎اَللّٰهُمَّ اَلْهِمْنِيْ رُشْدِيْ وَاَعِذْنِيْ مِنْ شَرِّ نَفْسِيْ

"O Allah! Inspire my heart to guidance and save me from the evil of my nafs (soul)." (Tirmidhi)

Dua #6

‎‎اَللّٰهُمَّ اِنَّكَ عَفُوٌّ تُحِبُّ الْعَفْوَ فَاعْفُ عَنِّيْ

"O Allah! You are the One who pardons, The Most Generous; You love to pardon, so pardon me." (Tirmidhi)

Dua #7

‎‎‎اَللّٰهُمَّ اِنِّيْ اَسْئَلُكَ الْهُدٰى وَالتُّقٰى وَالْعَفَافَ وَالْغِنٰى

"O Allah! Indeed I, I ask of You, guidance and piety and chastity and to be free of depending on anyone (except You)." (Muslim)

Dua #8

‎‎‎‎اَللّٰهُمَّ إِنِّيْ اَسْاَلُكَ عِلْمًا نَّافِعًا وَّرِزْقًاطَيِّبًا وَّعَمَلًا مُّتَقَبَّلًا

"O Allah! I ask You for knowledge which is beneficial, and sustenance which is pure, and deeds which are accepted." (Mishkat)

Dua #9

‎‎‎‎‎اَللّٰهُمَّ اجْعَلْ اَوْسَعَ رِزْقِكَ عَلَيَّ عِنْدَ كِبَرِ سِنِّيْ وَ انْقِطَاعِ

"O Allah! Expand for me Your provision till my old age and till the end of my life." (Haakim)

Dua #10

‎‎‎‎‎‎اَللّٰهُمَّ اكْفِنِيْ بِحَلَالِكَ عَنْ حَرَامِكَ وَ اَغْنِنِيْ بِفَضْلِكَ عَمَّنْ سِوَاكَ

"O Allah! Make sufficient for me what is halal (lawful) and to dispense with what is haram (unlawful) and enrich me with Your Favours so that I need no one but You." (Tirmidhi)

Dua: Supplication.

Sunnah: The prophet (SAW)'s precept/Prophetic teachings
